Wandering Jangseung

  • Animated 3D Design
  • 2025
  • Personal Project

Description

Inspired by the jangseung—a traditional Korean guardian spirit that wards off evil and misfortune. Once honored with offerings during village rituals, it now stands alone after the village has vanished. Still a protector at heart, it wanders the city, collecting clothes, accessories, and treasured belongings as modern-day offerings. To the jangseung, this isn't theft—it's the respect it still deserves for watching over others.
