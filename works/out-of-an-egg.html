Out of an egg

  • 2D Motion graphics
  • 2024
  • Team Project

Description

This project follows Woojoo, an aspiring film director who struggles with self-doubt after repeated failures and rejections. Entering the world of her own screenplay, she faces a giant spider that embodies her fears and anxieties. With newfound courage, Woojoo defeats the spider and breaks free from the “egg” that symbolizes the limits of her small world.

In doing so, she discovers new possibilities and a deeper sense of self. Inspired by themes from Demian, the work visualizes fear, transformation, and self-discovery through symbolic imagery and animation.
