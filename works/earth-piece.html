Earth Piece

  • 2D Motion Graphics
  • 2025
  • Personal Project

Description

This animation is inspired by Earth Piece: “Listen to the Sound of the Earth Turning”, an instruction piece by Yoko Ono. Though we cannot hear the Earth turning, its silent rotation marks the passage of time—through day and night, years and centuries.

Through five scenes—flower fields, forests, oceans, night skies, and deserts—I visualize how life thrives in some places yet fades in others. The final scene shows fragile grass pushing through desert sand, not as a hopeful start, but as a fragile continuation shaped by time's quiet, constant movement.
